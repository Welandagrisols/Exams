Zeraki Analytics is a data-driven digital platform designed for primary and secondary schools to automate, track, and analyze student academic performance. 
Here is exactly what it does and how it works:
What It Does
Automates Grading: Converts raw exam scores into official grades instantly based on customizable school or national grading scales.
Tracks Performance: Monitors the academic progress of individual students, specific classes, subject streams, and entire school terms over time. 
Generates Report Forms: Designs and compiles comprehensive student report cards automatically, eliminating manual paperwork for teachers. 
Sends SMS Notifications: Dispatches exam results, fee balances, and school announcements directly to parents' mobile phones. 
Identifies Trends: Highlights top-performing subjects, pinpoints struggling students, and reveals teaching gaps across different classes. 
How It Works
Data Entry: Teachers input raw student marks directly into the Zeraki Analytics system via a web browser or the mobile application. 
Cloud Processing: The data is securely uploaded to cloud servers, where relational databases (like MySQL/SQL Server) run automated formulas and calculations.
Instant Analytics: The system instantly processes the marks to calculate totals, averages, standard deviations, subject positions, and overall class rankings.
Dashboard Visualization: Principal, teachers, and administrators view the results through intuitive charts, graphs, and tabular dashboards. 
Distribution: Results are shared digitally with parents via the Zeraki Achievements app or automated SMS alerts.
